What's Included

Rambam:

A daily summary of Maimonides' teachings, covering practical Jewish laws and timeless ethical insights from his major works.

Daf Yomi:

A daily study of one page of Talmud, offering deep discussions on Jewish law, ethics, and philosophy, completed in a 7.5-year cycle.

Tanya Yomi:

A daily glimpse into the Tanya, the foundational book of Chabad Chassidut, guiding the soul in understanding G-d, faith, and inner growth.

Daily Halacha:

A practical, bite-sized teaching on Jewish law to guide daily living, from morning routines to broader religious practices.
